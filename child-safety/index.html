Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: June 1, 2026 Last Updated: June 6, 2026

My Steady Circle is committed to maintaining a safe platform and has zero tolerance for content or behavior that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors in any way.

1. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on My Steady Circle:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Content that sexualizes minors in any way
  • Any attempt to use the platform to groom, exploit, traffic, or abuse minors
  • Sharing, soliciting, or distributing content that endangers the safety of children

Violations of these standards will result in immediate account termination and reporting to the appropriate authorities.

2. Age Restrictions

My Steady Circle is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly permit minors to create accounts or use the platform. Account creation requires a valid email address and authentication through our identity provider, which we use to enforce our age restriction policy.

If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete their associated data.

3. How to Report Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior on My Steady Circle that you believe violates our child safety standards or endangers a minor, please report it immediately:

  • In-app: Use the report function available on user profiles and content within the app
  • Email: mysteadycircle@gmail.com — include as much detail as possible

We treat all child safety reports as urgent and will respond promptly.

4. Our Response to Reports

Upon receiving a child safety report, we will:

  • Review the report immediately
  • Suspend the reported account pending investigation
  • Preserve relevant evidence in accordance with applicable law
  • Report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) via the CyberTipline, as required by US federal law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A)
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations
  • Permanently terminate accounts found to have violated our child safety standards

5. Compliance

My Steady Circle complies with all applicable child safety laws, including but not limited to:

  • The PROTECT Our Children Act (mandatory reporting of CSAM to NCMEC)
  • The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Applicable state and international child safety laws

6. Contact

For child safety concerns, compliance questions, or to report a violation, contact our designated child safety point of contact:

Email: mysteadycircle@gmail.com
Please include "Child Safety" in the subject line. We treat all child safety reports as urgent.
