How it works

Three simple steps

No complicated setup. Invite your circle, record a check-in, and let the people who care about you know you're doing well.

1

Invite your circle

Choose the trusted people in your life — family, friends, supporters. Send them an invite link directly from the app.

2

Record a check-in

Tap record, say a few words, and share. Each video is delivered to your circle and automatically deleted within 24 hours.

3

Receive encouragement

Your circle members can send you short encouragement messages to lift you up throughout the day.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built for simplicity, designed for real connection.

🎥

Video Check-ins

Record a short video to let your circle know you're okay. Videos are private, shared only with the people you choose, and automatically deleted after 24 hours.

👥

Your Trusted Circle

You control who is in your circle. Invite people with a simple link, remove them anytime. No strangers, no algorithms — just the people you trust.

💕

Encouragement

Circle members can send you short uplifting messages. Messages are personal and ephemeral — they expire after 24 hours, keeping the feed fresh and meaningful.

📍

Last Known Location

When you record a check-in, your location is shared with your circle so they know where you were. No continuous tracking — just a single moment of connection.
